Topic: Payroll Management System

A Payroll Management System is a software project that automates and simplifies payroll processes within organizations. It manages employee compensation, tax deductions, benefits, and generates payslips.

Topic: A9G-Based Dementia Tracker

The Guardian Angel is an IoT-based device designed to assist individuals with dementia. It utilizes a GPS+GPRS+GSM module (A9G board) to track the user's location and make voice calls


Topic: Crime Reporting System

A crime reporting system is a technology-driven platform designed to allow citizens and law enforcement agencies to report, track, and manage criminal activities and incidents. These systems play a vital role in promoting public safety, increasing community engagement, and enhancing the efficiency of law enforcement efforts.
